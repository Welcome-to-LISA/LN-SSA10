# LN-SSA10

**Scientific data paper: “Annual 10 m Suaeda salsa distribution dataset in Liaoning Province, China, from 2019 to 2025”**

**Abstract**wfwef

Coastal salt marshes are essential for ecosystem stability and biodiversity conservation. *Suaeda salsa*, as a typical pioneer species, plays an important role in salinity regulation and carbon sequestration. Liaoning Province represents a major distribution region of *Suaeda salsa* in China and is characterized by high carbon emissions from heavy industry. Therefore, monitoring its spatiotemporal distribution is important for ecological conservation in Liaoning Province.

However, the high-resolution spatial distribution of *Suaeda salsa* in Liaoning in recent years remains unclear. In this study, we produced annual 10 m maps of *Suaeda salsa* for Liaoning Province from 2019 to 2025 (LN-SSA10), using Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2 data. Specifically, potential distribution regions were identified, followed by feature selection based on spectral separability and classification map generation using Random Forest with seasonal features. The LN-SSA10 maps achieved high classification accuracy, with user’s and producer’s accuracies of 0.98 and 0.97, respectively, and captured pronounced spatiotemporal dynamics of *Suaeda salsa* along the Liaoning coast. This dataset provides vital data support for coastal wetland monitoring and ecological restoration assessment.
